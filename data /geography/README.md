# Geography Data

This directory contains geospatial resources used by the Wildfire Decision Support System.

These datasets provide the geographic context required for spatial analysis, wildfire risk mapping and visualization of environmental indicators across the study area.

## Included Data

* Administrative boundaries
* Provincial polygons
* Fire management regions
* Geospatial reference layers
* Shapefiles and associated metadata

## Purpose

Geospatial data is used to:

* Delineate the study area
* Support wildfire risk mapping
* Visualize model predictions
* Aggregate risk metrics by region
* Enable spatial decision support

## Notes

Shapefiles are provided for demonstration and reproducibility purposes. Additional geographic layers may be obtained from official government and environmental agencies.

Large geospatial datasets are not included in this repository due to size constraints.
